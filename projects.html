Completed Project:
Modern Multi Cuisine Restaurant

We are delighted to present one of our recent completed projects, a stunning modern restaurant that stands as a testament to our commitment to innovative design and impeccable craftsmanship.

Nestled in a bustling urban setting, this restaurant exudes an air of sophistication and charm, inviting patrons to indulge in a culinary experience like no other.


  • Contemporary Design: The restaurant boasts a contemporary architectural design, characterized by clean lines, minimalist aesthetics, and an abundance of natural light. From the sleek exterior facade to the inviting interior spaces, every element is meticulously crafted to create a stylish and welcoming ambiance.

  • Outdoor Oasis: Enhancing the dining experience is a captivating outdoor oasis, complete with a tranquil fountain, lush garden, and cozy seating areas. Perfect for alfresco dining or casual gatherings, this outdoor space offers a serene retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

  • Innovative Concepts: Setting new standards in culinary innovation, the restaurant incorporates cutting-edge concepts and creative design elements. From a charming cafe setup to a convenient drive-in option, patrons can enjoy a variety of dining experiences tailored to their preferences.

  • Family-Friendly Environment: Catering to families and individuals alike, the restaurant features a dedicated family dining area, providing a comfortable and inclusive space for guests of all ages to enjoy quality time together.

Our Ongoing Project: The Charming Bungalow

Our current project is a beautiful bungalow situated in a peaceful location, blending old-fashioned charm with modern style. Designed as a two-story house, this 4BHK home combines classic and modern elements, tailored to suit the preferences of our client.


  • Swimming Pool and Courtyard: The bungalow features a lovely swimming pool, providing a refreshing spot amidst greenery. A calm courtyard offers a peaceful area, perfect for relaxation and quiet moments.

  • Japanese Influence: Inspired by Japanese design, this home includes elements of simplicity, cleanliness, and natural beauty. From sliding doors to Zen gardens, every detail reflects the timeless elegance of Japanese architecture.

  • Modern Amenities: Alongside traditional elements, the bungalow includes modern amenities designed to enhance comfort and convenience. From modern kitchens to a private theater room, every space is designed to meet modern lifestyle needs.

  • Creative Innovation: Our team is dedicated to bringing creativity and innovation to every aspect of this project. From unique architectural details to innovative design solutions, we aim to exceed expectations and create inspiring spaces.

"Having dreamt of my ideal home for over a decade, I entrusted Mr. Mohan Prasad with bringing my vision to life. Despite the distance, his professionalism and dedication have ensured that the project is progressing smoothly. I have full confidence in his ability to deliver excellence."

Mr Loganathan

House Owner

Throughout the project, we prioritize open communication and collaboration with our client to ensure their vision is realized. Every decision is made with their preferences and satisfaction in mind, resulting in a personalized living experience.

With a budget of 1 crore rupees, this project represents a significant investment in quality craftsmanship and design excellence. While progress continues steadily, our commitment to perfection remains unwavering, with an anticipated completion date in June 2024.

This ongoing bungalow project reflects our dedication to delivering exceptional homes that seamlessly blend luxury, comfort, and creativity. As we work towards its completion, we look forward to showcasing the timeless beauty and unmatched craftsmanship of this serene haven.
